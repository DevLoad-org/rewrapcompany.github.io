Returns & Shipping


Exchanges & Returns

We invest a great deal of time and care in curating and upcycling our products. As a company policy we do not provide refund on any product sold. Once the REWRAP pieces are sold they can be exchanged for replacement or a credit note (provided the aforementioned terms and conditions are met). If for some reason, we’ve missed an issue spot and you’d like to exchange/ return them, you can write to us at support@rewrap.store and someone from our customer care team will contact you within 1-2 business days and will guide you through the process of returning and exchanging the REWRAP pieces. They may request for further information / photographs to assess your return request. Please do assist them.

Disclaimer

Please note that not all products are eligible for exchange. All made-to-order and upcycled products are non-returnable and non-exchangeable. In order to maintain fairness to our Sellers, as well as keep our prices fair, many items are "Final Sale." Please make it a point to understand which products are eligible for returns before purchasing.

How to Return?

We accept returns, solely at our discretion, on damaged on unusable products. You must inform us within 3 days of delivery of the product to you with proof of such damage of the Product. Please send us an email to support@rewrap.store with your order number and attach an image of the piece purchased. Once you have mailed us, we will provide you with the address to which the Product has to be shipped. The cost for shipping the product back to us is to be borne by you. For all International exchanges and returns, the customer will have to bear all shipping charges as well as customs charges if applicable. Please ensure that the product is in its original condition and packaging. Item(s) which show signs of wear and/or being worn before will not be eligible for returns or exchanges. We would require all original papers to be returned with the product. REWRAP reserves all rights to inspect the condition of the product before processing any returns or exchanges. As a company policy we do not provide refund on any product sold. Once the REWRAP pieces are sold they can be exchanged for replacement with a credit note (provided the aforementioned terms and conditions are met). All custom made and up-cycling orders are non- returnable.

International Orders

We don't do free international shipping. The cost of the courier needs to be borne by the customer. Your card will be charged when your payment is confirmed and subsequently the package will be shipped to its final international destination. We try our best to ship our REWRAP pieces to you within 2 weeks. The shipping time for customized orders and out of stock products would however be longer and we would keep you posted about the approximate delivery date post ordering as well. These delivery times are subject to location, distance and our logistics partners. You may be charged for handling fees and taxes as your order passes through customs. Any charges on a parcel must be paid by the person receiving the parcel (this also applies to retail & wholesale customers). REWRAP has no control over these charges and we can’t tell you what the cost would be, as customs policies and import duties vary widely from country to country. It might be a good idea to contact your local customs office for current charges before you order, so you are not surprised by charges you were not expecting.

Terms & Conditions - Seller


Product(s) Acceptance

  • We shall only accept clean, quality merchandise in good condition and on consignment basis.
  • No product will be accepted by us without proper inspection and during the inspection period of five (5) working days, The Rewrap Company will not be responsible for any damage/defects found in the product(s).
  • On inspection if the product is found damaged The Rewrap Company shall return the same to you in an as is where is condition and you shall have no recourse to claim any amount whatsoever on the returned product.
  • After undergoing The Rewrap Company’s quality inspection the product(s) will be photographed and listed on our platform within ten (10) working days.
  • The Rewrap Company reserves the right to refuse listing of a particular product.
  • All listed products will be held by us on consignment basis for a minimum of three months. To be abundantly clear, as this is on consignment basis, there is no sale conducted between you and us with respect to any product and no amounts due from us to you until the product is sold to the end customer.

Determination of Price

  • The selling price of each Product will be determined by The Rewrap Company after inspection of the product.
  • After listing the Product for three months, we will have the option to list the same at a discounted selling price, determined by us, for a further period of two weeks.
  • On the sale of any of the listed products, you shall be entitled an agreed amount in percentage (the “Payout”) based the selling price or the discounted selling price of the product(s), as the case may be.
  • Our Payout calculations to you shall be as follows:
    • On items of the value Rs. 100 - Rs. 2,499 - 50% Payout to you on the amounts received
    • On items of the value Rs. 2,500 - Rs. 9,999 - 60% Payout to you on the amounts received
    • On items of the value Rs. 10,000 & above - 70% Payout to you on the amounts received
Please Note

  • All Payouts will be calculated on the final selling price exclusive of GST and after subtracting 18% GST charged on our commission, when applicable.
  • All payments to you shall be made based on the details of your PAN card, if required, and to the Bank Account provided by you to us. No payments shall be made in cash.
  • You will receive the Payout within 30 working days of the sale and delivery of your product(s), provided that the same is not returned by the end Customer.
  • If a Product is unsold even after discounting, then at your option, the product will be returned (shipping charges applicable) to you, retained by us to up-cycle (either for you or another customer), or donated to a charity of our choice.
Governing Law

Any dispute arising out the terms of this consignment agreement shall be governed by the laws of India and subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Courts in Chennai only.
